About PeerInsight

A second, independent set of eyes on your work

PeerInsight provides independent academic review services that help students strengthen their thesis or dissertation before submission. We read closely, flag what needs attention, and explain why — so you can revise with confidence, not guesswork.

  • Independent reviews, free from institutional pressure
  • Honest feedback delivered constructively
  • Practical, actionable suggestions for improvement
  • Confidential handling of every document
